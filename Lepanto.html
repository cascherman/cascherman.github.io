The details of this important historical event

  • The Battle of Lepanto was fought between the Ottoman Empire and The Holy League on on October 7, 1571
  • The Holy League was made up of a coalition of European countries, mainly the Spanish Empire and the Venetian Republic.
  • The battle was fought in the Gulf of Patras.
  • The Holy League was assembled by Pope Pius V.
  • The Ottoman fleet sailed out from Lepanto, and the Holy League set out from Messina, Sicily.
  • The King of Spain, Philip II, was the primary financier of the force and the Venetians supplied most of the ships.
  • The victory of the Holy League over the Ottoman Empire marked a major turning point in Ottoman expansion.
